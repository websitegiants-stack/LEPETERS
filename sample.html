Who We Are

Building Trust & Quality

A 100% black-owned construction powerhouse delivering exceptional results across South Africa for nearly two decades.

Transforming Visions Into Reality

Since 2007, LEPETERS Trading has stood as a beacon of excellence in the South African construction industry. Our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction has earned us the trust of countless residential and commercial clients.

As a proudly black-owned and controlled enterprise, we bring both traditional craftsmanship and modern expertise to every project we undertake. Our NHBRC certification ensures that every build meets the highest industry standards.
